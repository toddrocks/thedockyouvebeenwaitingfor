The waterfront

There are two kinds of waterfront. This is the kind you live on.

Roughly 130 feet of the property's southern edge is open Choctawhatchee Bay, facing due south. That orientation is the whole game: the sun tracks across the water all day long, so the view is lit from morning to evening rather than staring into a neighbor's roofline.
